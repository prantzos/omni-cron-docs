Privacy Policy for Feast

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Feast app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by OmniCron (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Paid service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Data Collection and Use

The Application is designed to be 100% offline-first.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or crash reports.

All data entered into the Application (including but not limited to settings, logs, and preferences) is saved directly to a local database stored securely on your device. The Service Provider has no access to this data.

Third-Party Access

Because the Application does not transmit data over the internet to our servers, no data is shared with third parties. The Application does not use third-party analytics or tracking frameworks.

User Control and Data Deletion

Since you own your data and it resides only on your local device, you have full control over it. You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting specific entries within the Application.
  • Clearing the Application's storage data via your device's operating system settings.
  • Uninstalling the Application. Uninstalling will permanently delete the local database and all associated data.

Exporting Debug Logs

The Application provides an optional, manual feature to export a debug log if you encounter technical issues. This log is generated locally on your device. It is entirely your choice whether to generate this log and email it to the Service Provider for support purposes. If you choose to email us a debug log, the contents of that email will be used strictly for troubleshooting and resolving the issue, and will not be shared with third parties.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
